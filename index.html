Bags of Hope is a student-led nonprofit club at Bellaire High School. We assemble goodie bags with toys, snacks, and comfort items — and deliver them to children at hospitals in the Texas Medical Center.

Our Mission

What We Do

We believe every child in a hospital deserves a moment of brightness. Here's how Bags of Hope makes that happen.

01

We Assemble

Club members hand-pack goodie bags with age-appropriate toys, stuffed animals, snacks, bottled water, and a personal note of encouragement.

02

We Give

Each bag is delivered directly to children at partnering hospitals in the Texas Medical Center, coordinated through hospital Child Life departments.

03

We Fundraise

Everything inside each bag is funded through donations from fellow students, families, and community supporters. Every dollar goes straight to the bags.

04

We Are Local

Rooted in Bellaire High School, we are a single-chapter organization focused on making a deep, meaningful impact right here in Houston.

The Process

How It Works

From a single donation to a delivered smile — here's how a bag comes to life.

Step 01

Donations Come In

Students and supporters donate through our GoFundMe page or at school fundraising events.

Step 02

We Shop & Gather

Club officers use funds to purchase approved items — toys, snacks, stuffed animals, and more.

Step 03

Members Assemble

At club meetings, members pack each bag by hand and add a personal handwritten card.

Step 04

Bags Are Delivered

We coordinate with the hospital's Child Life team and deliver bags directly to children in need.

Why We Do It

Crafting Smiles
Through Kindness

Hospital stays can be incredibly isolating for children. While medical teams focus on physical health, we believe emotional comfort is just as vital.

Our student-led initiative brings together volunteers to assemble care bags filled with toys, creative activities, and handwritten notes — ensuring no child feels scared or alone during their recovery.

No child should feel alone while they heal.
