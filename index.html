Bags of Hope is a student-led nonprofit club at Bellaire High School. We assemble goodie bags filled with toys, snacks, and comfort items — and deliver them to children at hospitals in the Texas Medical Center.

Our Mission

What We Do

We believe every child in a hospital deserves a moment of brightness. Here's how Bags of Hope makes that happen.

🎒

We Assemble

Club members at Bellaire High School come together to hand-pack goodie bags with age-appropriate toys, stuffed animals, snacks, bottled water, and a personal note of encouragement.

💛

We Give

Each bag is delivered directly to children at partnering hospitals in the Texas Medical Center — approved and coordinated through hospital Child Life departments.

🙌

We Fundraise

Everything inside each bag is funded through donations — from fellow students, families, and community supporters. Every dollar goes straight to the bags.

🏫

We Are Local

Bags of Hope is rooted in our Bellaire High School community. We are a single-chapter organization, focused entirely on making a deep, meaningful impact right here in Houston.

The Process

How It Works

From donation to delivery, here's how a goodie bag comes to life.

01

Donations Come In

Students and supporters donate through our GoFundMe or in-person at school events.

02

We Shop & Gather

Club officers use funds to purchase approved items — toys, snacks, stuffed animals, and more.

03

Members Assemble

At club meetings, members pack each bag by hand and add a personal handwritten card.

04

Bags Are Delivered

We coordinate with the hospital's Child Life team and deliver the bags to children in need.
